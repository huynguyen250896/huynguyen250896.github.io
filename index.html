About Me

Who I Am?

Hi! I am Huy. I am a 1st-year 2nd-year 3rd-year PhD student in Computer Science at Auburn University, advised by Prof. Wei-Shinn (Jeff) Ku. I have also been fortunate to receive mentorship from Dr. Zongliang Yue and Prof. Hao Chen. Previously, I had the privilege of working with Prof. Duc-Hau Le during my undergraduate studies.

My primary research focuses on development and application of statistical methods for multi-omics studies, including pathway analysis, bulk and single-cell analyses, genome-wide association studies, polygenic risk scores, and spatially resolved omics analyses of transcriptomics, metabolomics, and proteomics. I also address core AI challenges raised by these problems - multi-modal modeling, agentic reasoning, uncertainty quantification, etc.
